My Dream Trip to Japan

Ultimate Travel Guide

Trip Research

Japan is a blend of ancient tradition and futuristic innovation. Start by exploring the official Japan National Tourism Organization website for destination ideas, travel guides, and cultural history. For traveler reviews, hotel and restaurant recommendations, and activity suggestions, check out the Japan page on TripAdvisor. Also be sure to look at the current weather in Japan to plan appropriately.

Getting There & Staying There

Flight & Hotel Booking

For flights to Tokya, Osaka, or other cities, and a range of hotel options, browse Expedia for flights and vacation deals and Booking.com for places to stay in Japan

Transportation Info

Japan has one of the best public transportation systems in the world. You can check train schedules and buy Japan Rail Passes at Japan Rail Pass Official Site. For metro information in major cities, try Tokyo Metro, and for planning your routes, don't forget to explore Google Maps: Japan.
